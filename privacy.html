Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. The "No Data" Promise

We do not collect, store, or see any of your personal data (names, emails, etc.) on our own servers. Your game progress is stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party "Modular" Services

To provide features like ad-removal and gameplay rewards, we integrate industry-standard third-party SDKs. These services may collect device identifiers (like Advertising IDs) and IP addresses to function.

Current Active Providers:

  • Platform Services: Google Play Services / Apple App Store Connect (for basic app functionality).
  • In-App Purchases: Google Play Billing / Apple IAP (to process your "Remove Ads" or "Skins" purchases).
  • Advertising: [Pending Provider - e.g., AdMob or Unity Ads]. Note: No advertising identifiers are collected until an ad network is integrated.

3. Your Rights

Since we don't store your data, "deleting your data" is as simple as uninstalling the app or clearing the app cache. For third-party data, you can reset your Advertising ID in your device's System Settings.

4. Target Audience

This app is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.
